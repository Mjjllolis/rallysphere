What clubs receive

The full price of the ticket or item, paid out by Finix to the club's connected bank account. The platform fee is added on top of the item price and charged to the buyer.

Processing & chargebacks

Card processing is handled by Finix. Chargeback / dispute handling follows Finix's standard policies.
